Home

Space2Space was designed to help people around the world adapt and assimilate into new cutural and physical environments. We try our best to help make it as easy as possible. There are two paths for users to choose: Become a Host or Become an Applicant.



User creates a Profile

Based on the information inserted into the database each user is matched by religion, language and country of origin. The matches are then displayed on "Available RoomMates" page.


Users then can choose to contact each other through email.
